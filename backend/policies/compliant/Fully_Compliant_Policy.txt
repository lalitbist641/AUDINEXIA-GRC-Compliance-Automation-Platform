ABC CORPORATION - FULLY COMPLIANT DPDPA 2023 POLICY
===================================================

1. CONSENT OBLIGATION (DPDPA-1)
We obtain explicit, informed consent from all users before collecting personal data.
Users can withdraw consent at any time through their account settings or by email.
All consent actions are timestamped and stored in our consent management system.
Consent records are maintained for audit purposes.

2. NOTICE TO DATA PRINCIPAL (DPDPA-2)
A clear privacy notice is provided at the point of collection including:
- Purpose: Account management, service delivery, and communication
- Data categories: Name, email, phone, address, payment information
- Retention period: 3 years from last activity
- Data principal rights: Access, correction, erasure, grievance
- DPO contact: dpo@abccorp.com

3. PURPOSE LIMITATION (DPDPA-3)
Personal data is collected only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
Data will NOT be used for secondary purposes including AI training or marketing.
Purpose binding is enforced through technical controls.

4. DATA MINIMIZATION (DPDPA-4)
We collect only necessary, adequate, and relevant data for the specified purpose.
Quarterly data minimization reviews are conducted.

5. DATA RETENTION (DPDPA-5)
Personal data is retained for 3 years from last interaction.
After this period, data is automatically deleted or anonymized.
Annual retention reviews ensure compliance.

6. DATA PRINCIPAL RIGHTS (DPDPA-6)
Data principals have the right to:
- Access their personal data (response within 15 days)
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Erasure (right to be forgotten)
- Grievance redressal

7. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL (DPDPA-7)
Grievances can be filed at grievance@abccorp.com
Complaints are acknowledged within 48 hours.
Resolution provided within 15 business days.

8. DATA PROTECTION OFFICER (DPDPA-8)
Our DPO is appointed and can be contacted at dpo@abccorp.com
DPO is responsible for compliance monitoring and board liaison.

9. SECURITY SAFEGUARDS (DPDPA-9)
AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
RBAC access controls with least privilege.
MFA required for all administrative access.
Regular security audits conducted quarterly.

10. BREACH NOTIFICATION (DPDPA-10)
Breaches reported to Data Protection Board within 72 hours.
Affected principals notified within 24 hours for high-risk incidents.
Incident response team activated immediately.

11. CHILDREN'S DATA (DPDPA-11)
Verifiable parental consent required for children under 18.
Age verification performed at account creation.
Parental consent re-verified annually.

12. CROSS-BORDER TRANSFER (DPDPA-12)
Data stored and processed within India only.
International transfers require adequacy determination and SCCs.
