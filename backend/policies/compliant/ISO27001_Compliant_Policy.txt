ABC CORPORATION - FULLY COMPLIANT ISO 27001:2022 POLICY
=======================================================

A.5.1 INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Information security policy is documented, approved by management, and communicated to all employees.
Policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed.

A.5.2 POLICY REVIEW
Management reviews the information security policy annually.
Changes require management approval and are communicated to stakeholders.

A.5.3 SEGREGATION OF DUTIES
Conflicting duties are segregated to prevent unauthorized access or modification.
Dual control is implemented for critical functions.

A.6.1 ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
Information security roles and responsibilities are clearly defined.
CISO is responsible for overall security posture.

A.6.2 MOBILE DEVICE POLICY
All mobile devices must be encrypted.
Remote wipe capability is enabled.
Lost devices must be reported within 1 hour.

A.6.3 TELEWORKING POLICY
Remote access requires VPN connection.
Security awareness training is mandatory for remote workers.

A.7.1 SCREENING
Background checks are performed for all new hires.
Employment verification and reference checks are conducted.

A.7.2 SECURITY AWARENESS
Annual security awareness training for all employees.
Phishing simulations are conducted quarterly.

A.8.1 ASSET INVENTORY
Complete asset inventory maintained and updated quarterly.
Each asset has an assigned owner.

A.8.2 ACCEPTABLE USE
Acceptable use policy defines permitted and prohibited activities.
Employees acknowledge policy annually.

A.9.1 ACCESS CONTROL
Access follows least privilege principle.
Access rights are reviewed quarterly.

A.9.2 AUTHENTICATION
Strong password policy with 12-character minimum.
MFA required for all remote and administrative access.
