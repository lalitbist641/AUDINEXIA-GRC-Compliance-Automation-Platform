### SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION AND SCOPE

#### 1.1 Purpose
TechVision India Private Limited ("Company," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting the personal data of our data principals (employees, customers, vendors, and visitors) in compliance with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA 2023)** and other applicable privacy laws.

#### 1.2 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and consultants of TechVision India
- All third-party vendors and business associates processing data on our behalf
- All systems, applications, and processes that handle personal data
- All branches and offices across India

#### 1.3 Definitions
| Term | Definition |
|------|-------------|
| Data Principal | Individual whose personal data is being processed |
| Data Fiduciary | TechVision India (the entity determining purpose and means) |
| Personal Data | Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual |
| Consent | Voluntary, specific, informed, unambiguous indication of wishes |

---

### SECTION 2: CONSENT OBLIGATION (DPDPA-1)

#### 2.1 Obtaining Consent
We obtain **explicit, informed consent** from data principals before collecting or processing their personal data. Consent is obtained through:

- **Double opt-in mechanism** for digital consent collection
- **Signed consent forms** for offline/paper-based collection
- **Checkbox with clear affirmative action** (pre-ticked boxes are NOT used)

#### 2.2 Consent Withdrawal Mechanism
Data principals can **withdraw consent** at any time through:
- Account settings portal: https://privacy.techvision.com/withdraw
- Email request to: **privacy@techvision.com**
- Physical notice to: Data Protection Officer, TechVision India, 7th Floor, Cyber Towers, HITEC City, Hyderabad - 500081

Upon withdrawal, we cease all processing and delete personal data within 30 days.

#### 2.3 Record of Consent
All consent actions are:
- **Timestamped** with exact date and time
- **Logged** in our Consent Management System (CMS)
- **Auditable** for regulatory inspection
- **Stored** for duration of processing plus 3 years

### SECTION 4: PURPOSE LIMITATION (DPDPA-3)

#### 4.1 Specified Purpose
Personal data is collected only for **specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes** disclosed at the time of collection:

| Purpose | Data Used | Retention |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Employment management | Name, PAN, Aadhaar, bank details | 7 years post-employment |
| Customer service delivery | Name, address, contact, order history | 3 years post-service |
| Vendor payment processing | GST, PAN, bank account | 10 years as per tax law |

#### 4.2 No Secondary Use
We DO NOT use personal data for:
- AI model training without explicit consent
- Secondary marketing without additional permission
- Any purpose incompatible with original collection

> **Prohibited:** Using customer data for internal analytics beyond disclosed purpose

---

### SECTION 5: DATA RETENTION AND DELETION (DPDPA-4, DPDPA-5)

#### 5.1 Retention Periods

| Data Category | Retention Period | Deletion Method |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Employee records | 7 years after exit | Secure erasure |
| Customer data | 3 years after last interaction | Anonymization |
| Vendor data | 10 years (tax compliance) | Archival + restriction |
| Website logs | 90 days | Automatic purge |
| Consent records | Duration + 3 years | Secure deletion |

#### 5.2 Deletion Mechanism
Upon retention expiry or consent withdrawal:
- **Digital data:** Secure overwrite (DoD 5220.22-M standard)
- **Physical records:** Cross-cut shredding
- **Backup tapes:** Degaussing and physical destruction
- **Cloud storage:** API deletion with confirmation

#### 5.3 Data Minimization
We collect only **necessary, adequate, and relevant** data. Quarterly data minimization reviews identify and delete unnecessary fields.

### SECTION 6: DATA PRINCIPAL RIGHTS (DPDPA-6)

#### 6.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)
Data principals can **access** their personal data by:
1. Submitting request via: https://privacy.techvision.com/access
2. Response within **15 business days** (Section 12 requirement)
3. Free for first request annually

#### 6.2 Right to Correction
Request correction of inaccurate data via: https://privacy.techvision.com/correct
- Acknowledged within 48 hours
- Corrected within 10 business days
- Corrections notified to all recipients

#### 6.3 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
Request **deletion** via: https://privacy.techvision.com/delete
- Erasure within 30 days of verification
- Exceptions: Legal retention requirements
- Confirmation email sent upon completion

#### 6.4 Response Timeline
All requests are responded to within the **statutory timeline of 15 business days** as mandated by DPDPA Section 12.

### SECTION 9: SECURITY SAFEGUARDS (DPDPA-9)

#### 9.1 Technical Security Measures

| Control | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| **Encryption at Rest** | AES-256 for databases, file storage, backups |
| **Encryption in Transit** | TLS 1.3 for all network communications |
| **Access Control** | RBAC with least privilege principle |
| **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** | Required for all administrative access |
| **Network Security** | Next-gen firewall, IDS/IPS, network segmentation |
| **Anti-Malware** | Endpoint protection on all devices |

#### 9.2 Organizational Security Measures
- **Background verification** for all employees with data access
- **Confidentiality agreements** signed by all personnel
- **Regular security awareness training** (quarterly)
- **Access reviews** every 60 days

#### 9.3 Security Testing
- **Vulnerability scans:** Weekly automated scans
- **Penetration testing:** Annual third-party assessment
- **Security audits:** Quarterly internal audits

### SECTION 10: BREACH NOTIFICATION (DPDPA-10)

#### 10.1 Breach Response Procedure
Upon detection of a **personal data breach**:
1. **Immediate containment** by incident response team
2. **Assessment** of risk to data principals (within 24 hours)
3. **Notification to Data Protection Board** within 72 hours
4. **Notification to affected principals** within 24 hours if high risk

#### 10.2 Breach Notification Content
Notifications include:
- Nature of breach (what data, how many principals)
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken to mitigate
- Contact for further information

#### 10.3 Incident Log
All security incidents are logged in our Incident Management System (IMS) with:
- Timestamp of detection and resolution
- Root cause analysis
- Corrective action taken

### SECTION 11: CHILDREN'S DATA (DPDPA-11)

#### 11.1 Age Verification
We implement **verifiable parental consent** for individuals under 18 years:
- Age verification at account creation
- Parent/guardian email verification
- Consent confirmation via OTP to parent's registered mobile

#### 11.2 Parental Consent
Parental consent requires:
- Parent's government ID verification
- Digital signature or recorded verbal consent
- Re-verification annually

#### 11.3 Special Protection
Children's data is subject to **enhanced safeguards**:
- Restricted data collection (name, age, parent contact ONLY)
- No behavioral profiling or targeted advertising
- Stored with additional encryption layer

---

### SECTION 12: CROSS-BORDER TRANSFER (DPDPA-12)

#### 12.1 Data Localization
All personal data of Indian data principals is **stored and processed within India** on servers located in Mumbai and Chennai data centers.

#### 12.2 International Transfer Requirements
Any transfer outside India requires:
- **Adequacy determination** by Central Government
- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** approved by MeitY
- **Data Transfer Impact Assessment (DTIA)**
- **Explicit consent** from data principals for international transfer

#### 12.3 Current Transfer Status
| Country | Data Type | Basis | Status |
|---------|-----------|-------|--------|
| USA | Anonymized analytics | Consent | Limited |
| Singapore | Customer support | SCCs | Approved |
