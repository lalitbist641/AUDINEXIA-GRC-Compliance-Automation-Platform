TECHVISION INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
DATA PROTECTION POLICY

SECTION 1: CONSENT
We obtain explicit consent from users before collecting personal data. Users can withdraw consent at any time through account settings.

SECTION 2: NOTICE
We provide a clear privacy notice at the point of collection including purpose, retention period, and user rights.

SECTION 3: DATA RETENTION
Personal data is retained for 3 years and then deleted securely.

SECTION 4: SECURITY
We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Multi-factor authentication is required for all admin access.

SECTION 5: BREACH NOTIFICATION
Security breaches are reported within 72 hours to the authorities.

SECTION 6: DPO
Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at dpo@techvision.com
